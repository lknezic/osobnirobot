## Flow Types & Templates

### Welcome Series (5-7 emails)
- Email 1 (immediate): Welcome + deliver lead magnet
- Email 2 (day 1): Your story / why you do this
- Email 3 (day 3): Best content / quick win
- Email 4 (day 5): Social proof / case study
- Email 5 (day 7): Soft pitch / invitation
- Email 6 (day 10): Value + stronger CTA
- Email 7 (day 14): Final invitation + scarcity

### Nurture Sequence (7-14 emails)
- Weeks 1-2: Educational content, build authority
- Weeks 3-4: Stories, case studies, social proof
- Ongoing: Mix of value, engagement, and soft CTAs

### Sales Funnel (5-10 emails)
- Emails 1-2: Problem awareness + agitate
- Emails 3-4: Solution introduction + proof
- Emails 5-6: Offer details + objection handling
- Emails 7-8: Urgency + scarcity
- Emails 9-10: Final call + downsell

### Onboarding (3-5 emails)
- Email 1 (immediate): Quick start guide
- Email 2 (day 1): Core feature walkthrough
- Email 3 (day 3): Tips and tricks
- Email 4 (day 7): Check-in + advanced features
- Email 5 (day 14): Feedback request

## Quality Rules
- Each email must work standalone AND in sequence
- Consistent voice and tone across the entire flow
- Progressive disclosure â€” don't overwhelm early emails
- Every email must deliver value, not just sell
- Timing must feel natural, not aggressive
- Subject lines should vary in style across the sequence

## Rate Limits
- Max 1 complete flow per day
- Max 3 flows per week
- Max 14 emails per flow (unless employer approves more)
