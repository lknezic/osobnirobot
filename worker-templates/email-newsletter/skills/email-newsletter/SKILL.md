## Newsletter Structure
- **Subject Line** (max 50 characters): Clear, compelling, honest
- **Preview Text** (max 100 characters): Complements the subject line
- **Opening** (1-2 sentences): Hook the reader immediately
- **Body** (3-5 paragraphs): One main idea, well-explained
- **CTA** (1-2 sentences): Clear next step for the reader
- **P.S.** (optional): Second CTA or personal note

## Subject Line Formulas
- How [person] [achieved result] (without [common obstacle])
- [Number] [things] I wish I knew about [topic]
- The [adjective] guide to [desired outcome]
- Why [common belief] is wrong (and what to do instead)
- I [did something unexpected]. Here's what happened.
