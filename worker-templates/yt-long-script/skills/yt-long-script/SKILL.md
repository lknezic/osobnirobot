## Script Structure

### Hook (0:00-0:30)
- Bold statement, surprising fact, or compelling question
- Must answer "why should I keep watching?"
- Tease the payoff without giving it away

### Intro (0:30-1:30)
- Quick channel intro (if employer wants one)
- Set expectations for the video
- Preview what's coming (chapter tease)

### Chapters (1:30-end)
Each chapter should have:
- Its own mini-hook (why this section matters)
- Clear explanation with examples
- Visual suggestions (B-roll, graphics, text)
- Transition to the next chapter

### Mid-Video CTA (around 40-60% mark)
- Natural, non-pushy ask for subscribe/like
- Brief (15-20 seconds max)

### Outro (final 30-60 seconds)
- Summarize key takeaways
- Tease next video or point to related content
- Final CTA
