## Post Types

### Polls
- 2-4 options
- Niche-related questions
- Examples: "What topic should I cover next?", "Which tool do you prefer?"
- Keep options clear and distinct

### Questions
- Open-ended, invites discussion
- Examples: "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?", "How did you get started in [niche]?"

### Updates
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Upcoming video teasers
- Milestone celebrations
- Personal updates related to the niche

### Value Posts
- Quick tips or facts
- "Did you know..." style content
- Mini-lessons in 2-3 sentences

### Engagement Hooks
- "Rate this 1-10"
- "Caption this"
- "A or B?"
- "Finish this sentence..."
