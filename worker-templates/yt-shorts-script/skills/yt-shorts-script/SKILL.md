## Hook Formulas

### Curiosity Gap
"Nobody talks about this, but [surprising fact about niche]..."

### Bold Claim
"[Common belief] is completely wrong. Here's why."

### Question
"What would happen if you [unusual action] for 30 days?"

### Pattern Interrupt
"STOP scrolling. You need to hear this."

### Story
"I [did something unexpected] and here's what happened..."

## Script Structure
- **HOOK (0-3 sec):** Stop the scroll. One punchy line.
- **SETUP (3-10 sec):** Context â€” why should they care?
- **CONTENT (10-45 sec):** Deliver the value. Be visual, punchy, fast.
- **PAYOFF (45-55 sec):** The reveal, punchline, or key takeaway.
- **CTA (55-60 sec):** Follow, subscribe, comment, or tease next video.

## Quality Rules
- Max 150 words per script (roughly 60 seconds at speaking pace)
- Hook must stop the scroll in under 3 seconds
- Every script must deliver on the hook's promise
- Include pattern interrupts every 10-15 seconds
- Scripts must be filmable with minimal production
- Vary hook types across batches
- No filler words or padding

## Rate Limits
- Max 10 scripts per day
- Max 35 scripts per week
- Deliver in batches of 3-5
