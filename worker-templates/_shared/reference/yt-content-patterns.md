# YouTube Content Patterns
## Video Scripts, Hooks, Shorts, and SEO Optimization

**Purpose:** Reference for YouTube-related skills (long scripts, shorts scripts, community posts)
**Also useful for:** Any video content, podcast scripts, presentation structures

---

## VIDEO HOOK FORMULAS (First 5 Seconds)

**CRITICAL:** YouTube decides fate in 5 seconds. The hook determines if they keep watching.

### DATA HOOKS:
- "Made [$ Amount] this week [doing X]."
- "Lost [$ Amount] on this [project]. Here's what went wrong."
- "Closed [N] projects: [N] winners, [N] losers."
- "[Win rate]% success over [N] attempts."
- "[$ Amount] in [N] months doing [method]."

### PROBLEM HOOKS:
- "[Bad thing] happened. [Specific consequence]."
- "[Common tool] spiked to [level]. My [work] in trouble."
- "[Process] got [complicated]. [System] broke."
- "[Rolled/adjusted] [N] times, lost track of [data]."
- "[Metric] down [N]%. [Option A] or [Option B]?"

### CURIOSITY HOOKS:
- "This [thing] made more than my entire [time period]."
- "Made same mistake for [N] months. Just realized it."
- "Everyone's panicking. I'm doing the opposite."
- "One rule I never break [doing X]."
- "[Feature] I ignored cost me [$ Amount]."

### QUESTION HOOKS:
- "[Option A] or [Option B]?"
- "What's your target [metric] for [activity]?"
- "Ever lost track of [important data]?"
- "How do you [do X] across [large amount] fast?"
- "[Event]: panic or opportunity?"

### Pattern:
**[Specific Result/Problem] + [Promise of Value]**

Always: Data → Context → Promise in first 15 seconds total.

## VIDEO SCRIPT TEMPLATES

### Template #1: RESULT DOCUMENTATION (7-12 minutes)

**Timing Breakdown:**
```
[00:00-00:05] Hook — Result with one sentence
[00:05-00:15] Value Statement — What they'll learn
[00:15-01:30] Setup — Details and context
[01:30-04:00] Entry — Why this approach (reasoning)
[04:00-06:30] Management — What happened during
[06:30-08:00] Exit — Outcome and final numbers
[08:00-08:30] Lessons — What you'd do differently
[08:30-09:00] Recap + CTA
```

**Script Pattern:**
```
[HOOK] "[Result] [doing X]. Here's the exact breakdown."

[VALUE] "I'm going to show you why I entered, how I managed it,
and why I [made this decision]. Plus the one thing I'd do differently."

[SETUP] "Let me pull up the details. [Screen share]
[Walk through all specific numbers and parameters]"

[ENTRY] "So why this approach? Three reasons.
First, [data-backed reason]. Let me explain...
Second, [strategic reason]. My data shows...
Third, [personal conviction reason]."

[MANAGEMENT] "What happened over the next [N] days?
Day 1-3: [Status]. Position was [metric]. Not much movement.
Day 4-5: [Change]. I had [alert/target] set at [level].
Day 6: [Resolution]. [Show notification/result]."

[EXIT] "So I [closed/completed] it. [Show execution]
Final result: [Numbers]. [Return calculation].
More importantly, [resources] now free for [next project]."

[LESSONS] "What would I do differently? Honestly, [reflection].
[One specific thing]. Consistency over optimization."

[RECAP + CTA] "Quick recap: [Summary].
If you want [related value], [soft product mention].
Subscribe for weekly [content type] — wins, losses, all of it."
```

### Template #2: EDUCATIONAL EXPLAINER (8-12 minutes)

```
[00:00-00:05] Hook — Problem or question
[00:05-00:20] Value Statement — What they'll learn
[00:20-01:30] Concept introduction with analogy
[01:30-04:00] Real example with numbers
[04:00-07:00] Step-by-step process
[07:00-09:00] Common mistakes to avoid
[09:00-09:30] Recap
[09:30-10:00] CTA
```

**Key principles:**
- One concept per video
- Use analogies to simplify
- Real examples with specific numbers
- Show mistakes before solutions
- Actionable steps viewer can take today

---

### Template #3: WEEKLY RECAP (15-20 minutes)

```
[00:00-00:10] Hook — Total result for week
[00:10-00:30] Value Statement
[00:30-02:00] Context — What happened this week
[02:00-12:00] Item-by-item breakdown (~2 min each)
[12:00-14:00] One key lesson from the week
[14:00-15:00] Next week setup
[15:00-15:30] CTA
```

**Rules:**
- Show wins AND losses (transparency builds trust)
- One clear lesson per week
- Preview next week (builds anticipation)
- Engagement question at end

## YOUTUBE SHORTS / SHORT-FORM VIDEO (30-60 seconds)

### Format:
- Vertical 9:16 ratio (1080x1920)
- 30-60 seconds max
- Fast-paced, no fluff
- Text overlays help

### Script Structure:

```
[00:00-00:03] HOOK — One sentence, data or problem
[00:03-00:10] SETUP — Brief context (one sentence)
[00:10-00:45] LESSON — Quick insight with numbers (one main point only)
[00:45-00:60] PUNCHLINE/CTA — Wrap up + follow
```

### Topics That Work for Shorts:
- Quick results
- One rule explained
- Data comparisons
- Common mistakes
- Tool quick tips

### What NOT to Do in Shorts:
❌ Try to teach complex concept in 60 sec
❌ Too much text on screen
❌ Slow pacing
❌ Multiple topics (one point only)

## YOUTUBE SEO OPTIMIZATION

### Title Optimization (60-70 chars)

**Formula:** [Keyword] + [Outcome/Number] + [Context]

**Best Practices:**
- Front-load keywords
- Include numbers
- Use brackets for context: [Real Results] [Guide] [Demo]
- Create curiosity without clickbait

---

### Description (First 150 chars Critical)

**Formula:**
```
Line 1: Hook + what they'll learn
Lines 2-10: Timestamps for sections
Lines 11-15: Links (website, social)
Lines 16-20: Tags/keywords
```

**Example:**
```
Learn how I [achieved result] using [method]. Full breakdown with exact details.

Timestamps:
0:00 - Hook & Overview
0:15 - Setup
1:30 - Why I Did This
4:00 - Management
6:30 - Decision Point
8:00 - Lessons Learned

[Website link]
[Social links]

#keyword1 #keyword2 #keyword3
```

---

### Tags (5-10 relevant)

**Primary (always use):** Core niche keywords
**Secondary (video-specific):** Topic-specific keywords, names, methods

---

### Thumbnail Design

**Elements:**
1. **Text:** 3-5 words MAX
2. **Face (optional):** Emotion matching content
3. **Visual:** Chart, screenshot, or result
4. **Color:** Green (win), Red (loss), Blue (educational)

**Specs:**
- 1280x720 pixels
- JPG or PNG
- Under 2MB
- High contrast (mobile visibility)

---

### First 30 Seconds Structure

```
[00:00-00:05] HOOK — Grab attention with data/problem
[00:05-00:15] VALUE — What they'll learn specifically
[00:15-00:30] CREDIBILITY — Why listen to you (results, experience)
```

This determines if they keep watching.
