## THE 3-SECOND RULE

**CRITICAL:** All short-form platforms decide fate in 3 seconds. Your hook IS the content.

### DATA HOOKS (First 3 Seconds):
- "Made [$ Amount] in [N] days"
- "Lost [$ Amount] on this one"
- "[Win rate]% success over [N] attempts"
- "[$ Amount] in [N] months [doing X]"

### PROBLEM HOOKS (First 3 Seconds):
- "Stop [doing common thing]"
- "This mistake cost me [$ Amount]"
- "[Bad thing happened], here's what I did"
- "[Metric] down [N]%, don't panic"

### PATTERN HOOKS (First 3 Seconds):
- "I did this for [N] months before..."
- "Everyone does [X], I do [Y]"
- "This changed my [approach]"
- "One rule I never break"

### QUESTION HOOKS (First 3 Seconds):
- "[Option A] or [Option B]?"
- "What's your [setting/target]?"
- "Ever [lost track / messed up / missed] [something]?"

**Universal Pattern:** [Attention-grabber] + [Promise of value]

## SCRIPT TEMPLATES

### Template #1: RESULT VIDEO (30 sec)

```
[0:00-0:03] "[Result statement with numbers]"
[0:03-0:08] Show entry/setup (screenshot or visual)
[0:08-0:13] Show exit/outcome (screenshot or visual)
[0:13-0:23] "Why I [made this choice]" + 3 quick reasons with text
```

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC VOICE

### TikTok Voice:
- **Ultra-fast:** No time for fluff
- **Visual:** Text overlays, screenshots
- **Value-first:** Immediate insight
- **Authentic > polished:** Real, not produced
- **Anti-guru:** Humble, peer

### Instagram Voice:
- **Visual-first:** Let data/images speak
- **Clean design:** Professional not corporate
- **Quick value:** Fast insights
- **Personality:** Show behind-the-scenes
- **Hashtag strategic:** 5-8 relevant

### YouTube Shorts Voice:
- Same as TikTok but slightly more polished
- Can reference longer videos for depth
- "Link in bio for full video" works

## PRODUCTION TIPS

### Text Overlays:
- Essential on all platforms
- Readable at mobile size
- 3-5 words per overlay
- High contrast against background

### Pacing:
- New visual every 2-3 seconds
- Cut dead air ruthlessly
- Move fast â€” the viewer will rewatch if interested

### Audio:
- Trending sounds boost discovery (TikTok, Reels)
- Voiceover works on all platforms
- Music optional but helps retention

### Visual Priority:
Instagram > TikTok > YouTube Shorts (visual expectations)
